    Welcome to the Cargolux Application Form! We're happy to see your interest in joining our team! There are three steps in our recruitment process:


    Application Form Written Entrance Exam mini practical (you will be asked to do as asked anytime from when you join our VA)


    Requirements:

    1) Be 13 years of age or older.

    2) Be Grade 3 or higher.

    3) Have a valid Infinite Flight Community (IFC) account.

    4) Have a valid Infinite Flight Pro Subscription.

    5) Have a minimum of 100 flight hours on Infinite Flight.

    6) Have a maximum of 30 Level 1 violations and 2 Level 2 & 3 violation in 1 year.

    7) Have a minimum of 20 landings in the last 90 days.
